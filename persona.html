How we build

Two pipelines. One evidence-aware schema.

The corpus is not a single undifferentiated dataset. Generation method, source context, confidence, and evidence boundaries matter.

01 · SYNTHETIC

Structured generation

Personas are sampled from a typed attribute graph, with compatibility rules designed to reduce contradictory combinations and preserve diversity.

  • Schema-constrained attributes
  • Conditional consistency rules
  • Reproducible sampling and rendering
02 · HUMAN-EVIDENCE

Grounded extraction

Human-authored public traces are mapped to supported schema attributes. Unobserved fields should remain unknown rather than being invented.

  • Evidence attached to assignments
  • Confidence and provenance tracking
  • Source-specific extraction policies
