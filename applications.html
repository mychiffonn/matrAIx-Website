A growing library of evaluation tasks that combine persona populations, interactive environments, telemetry, and task-specific metrics. The examples below are placeholders for the public task registry.

Product & evaluation domains

Four initial domains where MatrAIx can evaluate products, features, and user-facing workflows with simulated users.

Domain 01

Commerce & Retail

Evaluate discovery, pricing, checkout, recommendations, customer support, and retention experiences.

Domain 02

Software & Developer Tools

Evaluate onboarding, documentation, coding assistants, technical workflows, and collaboration products.

Domain 03

Financial Services

Evaluate account opening, support, financial guidance, trust, compliance, and safety workflows.

Domain 04

Healthcare & Digital Health

Evaluate navigation, booking, patient communication, adherence, and accessible digital-health experiences.

Domain describes what product or experience is being evaluated. Task type describes how the simulated user interacts with it.
