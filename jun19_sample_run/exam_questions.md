# English and Spanish Exam Questions

## English Proficiency Exam

### Section 1: Reading Comprehension

**Question 1.1** — Easy
```
Read the passage and answer the question:

"The library is a quiet place where people go to read books and study. 
Students come here to prepare for exams. Teachers use the library resources 
to prepare lessons. Everyone must keep the library clean and quiet."

What do students come to the library for?
A) To sleep
B) To prepare for exams
C) To meet friends
D) To watch movies
```

**Question 1.2** — Medium
```
Read the passage and answer the question:

"Climate change is one of the most pressing challenges of our time. 
Rising temperatures are causing polar ice to melt, which leads to higher 
sea levels. Many cities near the coast are at risk. Scientists recommend 
reducing carbon emissions to slow this process."

According to the passage, what causes higher sea levels?
A) Pollution in the ocean
B) More rain and snow
C) Polar ice melting
D) Warmer ocean currents
```

**Question 1.3** — Difficult
```
Read the passage and answer the question:

"The advent of artificial intelligence has fundamentally transformed 
how we approach complex problems. Machine learning algorithms can now 
process vast quantities of data in milliseconds, enabling predictive 
analytics that were previously impossible. However, this technological 
advancement raises ethical concerns about data privacy and algorithmic bias."

What does the passage suggest about machine learning?
A) It is slower than human analysis
B) It can process large amounts of data quickly
C) It has solved all ethical problems
D) It is only used in academic research
```

### Section 2: Vocabulary & Grammar

**Question 2.1** — Easy
```
Choose the correct word to complete the sentence:

"I _______ to the museum last weekend."
A) go
B) goes
C) went
D) going
```

**Question 2.2** — Medium
```
Choose the correct word to complete the sentence:

"If she _______ known about the meeting, she would have attended."
A) would have
B) had
C) has
D) have
```

**Question 2.3** — Difficult
```
Choose the correct word to complete the sentence:

"The CEO's _______ approach to management has _______ the company's 
corporate culture significantly."
A) progressive / transformed
B) regressive / stagnated
C) conservative / abolished
D) ambiguous / clarified
```

### Section 3: Writing

**Question 3.1** — Easy
```
Write a short response (2-3 sentences):

"What is your favorite hobby and why?"
```

**Question 3.2** — Medium
```
Write a short response (3-4 sentences):

"Describe a memorable experience from your past. When did it happen? 
Where were you? Who was with you?"
```

**Question 3.3** — Difficult
```
Write a paragraph (5-7 sentences):

"Do you think remote work is better than working in an office? 
Present your opinion and provide at least two reasons to support it."
```

## Spanish Proficiency Exam

### Section 1: Comprensión de Lectura (Reading Comprehension)

**Pregunta 1.1** — Fácil
```
Lee el pasaje y responde la pregunta:

"El café es una bebida popular en muchas partes del mundo. La gente 
bebe café por la mañana para despertarse. También se bebe después 
de las comidas. El café tiene cafeína, que nos da energía."

¿Por qué la gente bebe café por la mañana?
A) Para dormir
B) Para despertarse
C) Para enfriarse
D) Para cocinar
```

**Pregunta 1.2** — Medio
```
Lee el pasaje y responde la pregunta:

"La música es una forma universal de expresión artística. Diferentes 
culturas tienen sus propios estilos musicales únicos. La música clásica, 
el jazz, el rock y el pop son algunos géneros populares. La música puede 
afectar nuestras emociones de manera profunda."

¿Qué es lo que la música puede hacer según el pasaje?
A) Solo entretener a la gente
B) Afectar nuestras emociones
C) Reemplazar el habla
D) Curar enfermedades
```

**Pregunta 1.3** — Difícil
```
Lee el pasaje y responde la pregunta:

"La sostenibilidad ambiental se ha convertido en una prioridad global. 
Las fuentes de energía renovable como la solar y la eólica ofrecen 
alternativas viables a los combustibles fósiles. Sin embargo, la 
transición energética requiere inversión significativa en infraestructura 
y cambios de comportamiento a nivel social."

¿Cuál es un desafío mencionado en la transición energética?
A) La energía solar es demasiado cara
B) No existen alternativas a los combustibles fósiles
C) Requiere inversión en infraestructura
D) Es imposible cambiar el comportamiento social
```

## Difficulty Scale

| Level | Type | Approx. Time | Description |
|-------|------|--------------|-------------|
| Easy | Basic vocabulary & simple past tense | 2-3 min per Q | Everyday topics, basic sentence structure |
| Medium | Complex sentences, conditional tense | 3-5 min per Q | Abstract concepts, intermediate grammar |
| Difficult | Advanced grammar, nuanced passages | 5-8 min per Q | Sophisticated vocabulary, subjunctive mood |

## Notes for Test Administration

- **Total Estimated Time**: 45-60 minutes per language exam
- **Target Audience**: Intermediate (A2-B1) to Advanced (B2-C1) learners
- **Scoring**: 
  - Multiple choice: 1 point each
  - Writing sections: Rubric-based (0-5 points based on grammar, vocabulary, organization)
- **Expected Performance**: 
  - Chinese-only speaker: Expected difficulty completing English/Spanish sections
  - Language barrier markers: Hesitation time, translation attempts, incomplete responses
